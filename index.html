The Problem

There are a number of people that are busy with their work and/or socializing with their friends and relatives that there are times where it is hard to find time to do the grocery. Our application rectifies that problem.

Why GroceLine

GroceLine makes it easier to do the grocery. Our app would allow a person to select the brand, quantity, and the store of a desired product online. It then dispatches someone to the customer's address to deliver the items that they ordered. All of this in just a push of a button.

Features

  • Enables users to order groceries online
  • Has a checkout feature that will show the order summary and total bill
  • Enables users to pay only when their tiems have arrived, via cash on delivery
